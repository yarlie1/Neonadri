# Cost Support Moderation Flags

## High-priority flags

Send a post or message to review when cost support appears together with:

- offers of money for time or attendance
- companionship language
- romantic or sexual quid pro quo language
- repeated cash offers unrelated to the meetup activity

## Pattern flags

Review when one or more of these patterns appear:

- unusually high cost support amounts
- repeated high-value posts from the same host
- specific gender targeting combined with high cost support
- late-night meetup plus private location plus high cost support
- text such as `pay you`, `cash`, `allowance`, `reward`, `for your time`

## Enforcement guidance

- remove or edit posts that frame money as payment for attendance or companionship
- warn or suspend repeat offenders
- preserve relevant post, chat, and account context for safety review
- route urgent cases into the safety escalation workflow
