# Safety Policy Draft

Last updated: 2026-04-22
Status: internal release draft for legal review

## 1. Safety scope

Neonadri is designed to support adult meetup discovery, not to guarantee personal safety. The product may offer safety tools and reduced public detail, but users should still use personal judgment in any interaction or meetup.

## 2. Current product safety measures

Current or planned safety measures may include:

- adults-only access language and age confirmation
- block tools
- limited or reduced public location detail before a match
- safety review and moderation workflows
- restrictions around unsafe cost support behavior
- limited post-meetup chat windows

## 3. Unsafe behavior examples

Unsafe behavior may include:

- threats or coercion
- stalking or repeated unwanted contact
- requests for money unrelated to direct meetup costs
- offers of money for attendance or companionship
- sexual or romantic quid pro quo language
- deceptive or misleading meetup setup
- suspected underage access

## 4. Safety expectations for users

Users should:

- meet only if they feel comfortable doing so
- pay attention to product warnings and status cues
- avoid sharing more personal information than needed
- avoid sending money for reasons unrelated to direct meetup costs
- use block or report tools where available

## 5. Safety review

Neonadri may review meetup, account, and message-related context where reasonably necessary to investigate unsafe behavior, enforce product rules, or respond to urgent risk.

## 6. Escalation

High-risk safety issues should be escalated internally for prompt review. Urgent threats, coercive conduct, underage concerns, or compensation-for-meeting behavior may require immediate restriction of content or account access.
