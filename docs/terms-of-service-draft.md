# Terms of Service Draft

Last updated: 2026-04-22
Status: internal release draft for legal review

## 1. Service scope

Neonadri is an adults-only meetup platform that helps users discover, host, and join low-pressure in-person meetups.

The service is intended for adults who want to coordinate real-world social activities such as coffee, meals, walks, study sessions, and similar meetups.

## 2. Eligibility

You must be 18 or older to use Neonadri.

By creating or using an account, you confirm that:

- you are at least 18 years old
- the information you provide is accurate
- you will use the service only for lawful personal use

We may suspend, restrict, or remove accounts we believe are underage or misrepresenting eligibility.

## 3. Account responsibility

You are responsible for:

- maintaining control of your account
- keeping your password secure

- promptly updating inaccurate profile information

You may not share your account with another person.

## 4. Meetup content and conduct

You are responsible for the meetups, profile content, messages, and reviews you create.

You may not use Neonadri to:

- harass, threaten, or stalk another person
- impersonate another person or misrepresent your identity
- post misleading meetup details
- arrange illegal activity
- offer money for attendance, time, companionship, or personal interaction
- request or offer romantic or sexual compensation
- evade a block, moderation action, or account restriction

## 5. Cost support rule

Any cost support shown on Neonadri may only refer to direct meetup-related costs such as food, drinks, tickets, transport, parking, or similar activity expenses.

Cost support may not be used to offer payment for:

- attendance
- time
- companionship
- personal interaction

We may remove or restrict content that appears to frame money as compensation for meeting someone.

## 6. Location information

To reduce safety risk, Neonadri may limit how precisely location details are shown before a match is confirmed.

Approximate or reduced location details may be shown publicly, while more detailed location information may only be visible after a match or later in the meetup flow.

Chat availability may be limited by product rules, including a post-meetup closing window.

You should use caution when interacting with other users or attending any in-person meetup.
