# Community Guidelines Draft

Last updated: 2026-04-22
Status: internal release draft for legal review

## 1. Purpose

Neonadri is meant for calm, adult, in-person social discovery. Use the platform in ways that keep meetups clear, respectful, and safe.

## 2. What we want on Neonadri

Use Neonadri to:

- host real meetup activities with clear details
- communicate honestly about timing, place, and expectations
- treat other users with respect
- keep cost support tied only to direct meetup expenses

## 3. What is not allowed

Do not:

- harass, intimidate, threaten, or stalk another person
- pressure someone into a meetup or conversation
- impersonate another person
- lie about meetup details, availability, identity, or age
- post sexual services, escort-style offers, or companionship-for-pay arrangements
- offer money for attendance, time, companionship, or personal interaction
- request off-platform cash for attending a meetup
- try to evade a block or moderation action
- use the service if you are under 18

## 4. Cost support rule

Cost support is only for direct meetup costs such as:

- food or drinks
- tickets or entry fees
- transport or parking
- other direct activity expenses

It is not for:

- paying someone to show up
- paying for time or presence
- paying for companionship
- romantic or sexual access

## 5. Location and safety expectations

Do not post or pressure others to share more location detail than the product is meant to reveal at that stage.

Respect privacy boundaries and meetup only in ways that are lawful and safe.

## 6. Reporting and blocking

If available in the product flow, use reporting or blocking tools when you encounter unsafe behavior, inappropriate payment requests, or abusive conduct.

## 7. Enforcement

We may remove content, limit visibility, restrict account features, suspend accounts, or ban users who violate these guidelines.
