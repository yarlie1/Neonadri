# Cost Support Guidelines

## Allowed use

`Cost support` may only describe direct meetup expenses tied to the activity itself, such as:

- food or drinks
- tickets or entry fees
- transport or parking
- other direct activity costs

## Not allowed

Do not use cost support to offer money for:

- attendance
- time
- companionship
- personal interaction
- romantic or sexual access

## User-facing product rule

Wherever the product shows `Cost support`, it should be paired with language that makes the limit clear:

- `For direct meetup costs only.`
- `Do not offer money for attendance, time, or companionship.`

## Copy rule

Avoid user-facing copy such as:

- `benefit`
- `reward`
- `allowance`
- `payment for joining`
- `pay you directly for meeting`

Prefer:

- `Cost support`
- `Direct meetup costs`
- `Host covers`
- `For food, tickets, or transport`
